Consumable Content

Art meets life.

Items you create as Ownables can be designed so that they get used up or perish. Choose wisely, as on the blockchain, once it's gone, it's gone forever...

For example, one Ownable could represent a character in a metaverse, while another represents a specific skin for that character. To equip the skin in-game, it needs to be 'consumed' by the character Ownable, essentially merging it with the character and making it a part of the character's Ownable.

  • Perishable Items
  • Equip Assets
  • Upgrades to Collectibles
